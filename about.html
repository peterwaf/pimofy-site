Who We Are

Pimofy Digital is a specialist data operations partner for SaaS and Ecommerce companies. Based in Nairobi, Kenya, we serve clients across the UK, Europe, and the United States.

Our team is built from BPO veterans who have processed millions of records and managed complex data workflows for companies that demand precision and speed. We have seen what good operations look like at scale—and we have built Pimofy to deliver exactly that, without the overhead of hiring, training, and managing an in-house team.

We deliberately made a choice that most outsourcing providers do not: to specialize. Rather than offer a little of everything, we focus on dedicated data operations. That focus is what lets us guarantee 98% quality accuracy and onboard only three new clients a month.

Why We Started Pimofy

We kept seeing the same pattern in growing companies: skilled, expensive people buried in repetitive data work. CRMs going stale. Backlogs growing faster than teams could clear them. Quality slipping through to customers. Managers firefighting instead of leading.

The issue was rarely a delivery problem. It was a capacity problem—and hiring more full-time staff was a slow, costly way to solve it. Pimofy was built to give those teams a better option: a dedicated, managed data operations capability that flexes with their volume, holds a consistent quality standard, and requires zero micromanagement.

What We Believe

Specialization beats generalization

Doing one thing exceptionally well serves clients better than doing many things adequately.

Capacity is the real product

Clients buy the capacity to serve more customers, launch faster, and grow without overwhelming their core team.

Quality is a system

Agreed standards, SOPs, QA oversight, and weekly reporting make accuracy repeatable—not good intentions.

Discretion is non-negotiable

We operate under your confidentiality and NDA requirements as standard, treating every record as our own.
