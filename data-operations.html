Our Guarantee & Quality Standards

Quality standards—target accuracy, turnaround times, and KPIs—are agreed in writing during Phase 1, so “good” is never ambiguous.

If standards are not met

At no additional cost, we provide additional training to the assigned specialist or replace the specialist entirely, and re-perform the affected work to the agreed standard.

If errors originate from our end

We correct and re-process the affected work at no cost, credit the associated hours back to your monthly capacity, and add a root-cause note to your SOPs so it cannot recur.

If errors originate from the client side

We flag the issue promptly, confirm the correct approach before proceeding, handle corrective rework within your existing hours, quote transparently for anything beyond your monthly capacity, and update the SOP together.
